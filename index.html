Fields of Time

The Fields of Time is a community art project run by the St Mary's Preservation Trust.

We are creating a tapestry which records the field-names of South Ronaldsay's south parish.

You can browse the field-names collected so far and view them on our map:

Browse List View Map

If you would like to volunteer to help collect the remaining field-names or to help with stitching, please get in touch.
